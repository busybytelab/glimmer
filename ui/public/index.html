Welcome to Glimmer

A simple, self-hosted app helping kids practice specific skills

About

Glimmer helps kids (ages ~6-12) practice skills like grammar and basic math through exercises generated by Large Language Models (LLMs) and reviewed/modified by parents.

Features

  • Parent-Configured: Create practice topics tailored to your child's needs
  • AI-Generated Content: Fresh practice materials created automatically
  • Privacy-Focused: Self-hosted for full control of your data
  • Child-Friendly: Simple interface designed for tablets and computers
